About This Project

Riding a motorcycle is about freedom, passion and live. But it is also about taken calculated risk!

Our project attempts shed light on many myths about the risks of riding motorcycles. We want to educate current and future motorcycles riders about the real risks and the actions that may considerably mitigate thoses risks analysing availabe data.

What We Do?

We use data to learn and predict future outcomes on whatever question where we want put our atention.

We ensure you that our data sources are reliable and our analysis strong.
